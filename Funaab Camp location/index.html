DEEPER LIFE CAMPUS FELLOWSHIP, CAMP LOCATION, FUNAAB CHAPTER.


Deeper Life Campus Fellowship, Camp Location, FUNAAB CHAPTER, is among the other DLCF locations in FUNAAB. Located behind Mechanic Village, Ibadan to Abeokuta road, Camp, Abeokuta
Deeper Life Campus Fellowship, Camp Location,Funaab Chapter,is a vibrant religious organization on the campus of The Federeal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta, Ogun State. Deeper life Campus Fellowship, Camp location is a place of worship that creates a chance for a deeper relationship with God. We are led by the teaching and inspiration of the Holy Spirit, we are taught to serve God, build character, and succeed academically. The fellowship's main purpose is to instill in members a closer and daily relationship with God while living a holy life and excelling academically.
